Product Introduction – 10nm Bandwidth Series Narrowband Bandpass Interference Filter (OD5, High Blocking & High Transmission)

Overview

The 10nm Bandwidth Series narrowband bandpass interference filter is a high-performance optical component designed for precise wavelength selection. With a full width at half maximum (FWHM) of 10nm, this filter transmits light within a very narrow spectral range while blocking all other wavelengths. Featuring an optical density (OD) of 5 (OD5) in the rejection band, it provides excellent blocking efficiency (99.999% attenuation), combined with high transmission (typically >90%) at the target wavelength. Manufactured using advanced ion-assisted deposition (IAD) or magnetron sputtering technology, these filters offer superior environmental stability, sharp edge transitions, and long-term reliability.

Key Features

1. Narrow Bandwidth (10nm FWHM)

  • Precisely selects a 10nm spectral window, enabling high-resolution spectral analysis and laser line purification.

  • Ideal for applications requiring strict wavelength discrimination, such as fluorescence microscopy, Raman spectroscopy, and LiDAR.

2. High Blocking Depth (OD5)

  • Achieves optical density ≥5 (OD5) across the entire rejection band, corresponding to >99.999% light blocking.

  • Effectively eliminates out-of-band stray light and background noise, ensuring superior signal-to-noise ratio (SNR).

3. High Transmission (T>90% at peak)

  • Peak transmittance typically exceeds 90%, maximizing optical signal throughput and minimizing power loss.

  • Balances high blocking with high transmission for demanding optical systems.

4. Steep Edge Roll-Off

  • Transition width from passband to rejection band is typically <1% of center wavelength (CW), providing excellent spectral contrast.

5. Durable Construction

  • Substrate materials: high-quality optical glass (e.g., B270, fused silica) or UV-grade quartz.

  • Hard-coated dielectric layers resist humidity, temperature cycling, and mechanical abrasion.

  • Compliant with environmental durability standards (MIL-C-48497, Telcordia GR-1221-CORE).

Applications

  • Fluorescence Microscopy – Excitation and emission filtering for multi-color imaging.

  • Raman Spectroscopy – Laser line cleanup and Rayleigh rejection filters.

  • Lidar & Atmospheric Sensing – Isolating specific laser return signals.

  • Biomedical Instrumentation – Flow cytometry, DNA sequencers, and clinical analyzers.

  • Machine Vision – Wavelength-specific illumination for defect detection.

  • Optical Communications – DWDM channel separation and noise filtering.

  • Astronomy – Narrowband imaging of celestial objects (e.g., H-alpha, OIII, SII).

  • Laser Systems – Harmonic separation and beam purification.
